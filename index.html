Instructions

  1. This is a two player game. Each player is represented by a game board above.
  2. Each board has 5 hidden ships randomly stationed either horizontally or vertically on 10x10 grid.
  3. The ships include:
    • Carrier - 5 blocks long
    • Battleship - 4 blocks long
    • Submarine - 3 blocks long
    • Cruiser - 3 blocks long
    • Dingy - 2 blocks long
  4. On their turn, the player will attempt to 'hit' their opponents ship by clicking on a square in their oppenents board.
  5. A successful 'hit' will be signified by an explosion in the targeted square. The player can continue firing until they 'miss.'
  6. A miss will be signified by a white X in the targeted square and ends the players turn.
  7. To 'sink' a ship, the player must 'hit' all squares that ship occupys.
  8. The first player to 'sink' all of their oppenents ships wins!
